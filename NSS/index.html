About Us

he National Service Scheme is an Indian government-sponsored public service program conducted by the Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports of the Government of India. Popularly known as NSS, the scheme was launched in Gandhiji's Centenary year in 1969. Aimed at developing student's personality through community service, NSS is a voluntary association of young people in Colleges, Universities and at +2 level working for a campus-community Volunteers generally work in villages, slums and voluntary agencies to help people in various ways like spread awareness about education,sanitation ,government schemes etc. We also try to help them in any other problem they are facing like if they have not recieving their proper ration we help them getting it. We also try to help them get employment based on their skill level.We also teach children computer and basic subjects .
